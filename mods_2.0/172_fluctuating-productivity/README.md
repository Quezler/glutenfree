Every tick a random recipe from the player force gains or loses up to 10% productivity, clamped in the 0% to 300% range.

Technologies cannot unlock recipe productivity while this mod is installed, productivity modules still function normally.

Made for the Foundry modding jam in may with the theme "Analysis Paralysis", this mod will nicely wreck your fancy ratios.

(only the "player" force is supported out of the box, and updating your mods might temporarily reset productivity to zero)
